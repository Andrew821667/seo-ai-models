An investment is an asset or item acquired to generate income or gain appreciation. Appreciation is the increase in the value of an asset over time. It requires the outlay of a resource today, like time, effort, and money for a greater payoff in the future, generating a profit . Key Takeaways An investment involves using capital in the present to increase an asset's value over time. Investment may include bonds, stocks, real estate, or alternative investments. Investments can be diversified to reduce risk, though this may reduce the amount of earning potential.
